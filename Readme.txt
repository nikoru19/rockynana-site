About Me
rockynana is a strong, independent cat who tries to find the good in life, even when things get messy. Here, you’ll discover joyful finds, personal reflections, helpful tips, and inspiring stories from people she admires.

Brand Identity
Concept: rockynana = a resilient, kind, independent cat who finds the good in life despite messy circumstances.
Mission Statement: “rockynana celebrates small joys, promotes good work, and spreads positivity—while helping brands and creators share their value with the world.”
Tone & Voice: Warm, personable, slightly quirky, authentic, positive but realistic.

Visual Identity:
Mascot/illustration of rockynana (a cat)
Subtle motifs of luck (7, charms)
Clean, simple, inviting design

Content Pillars
Things That Made Me Smile – Positive work & creative finds
rockynana’s Ramble – Personal experiences & life reflections
rockynana’s Picks – Helpful lists & recommendations
Humans of rockynana – Inspiring interviews / real stories
